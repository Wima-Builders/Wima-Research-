Section D

Master Craftsmen Partnership Programme

Connecting skill-rich, marketing-poor craftsmen to Wima's existing client pipeline. 8 craft categories. 15–20% coordination fee on every project. The Mon Artisan model applied to Ghana's premium home crafts market.

The West Africa Precedent — Mon Artisan, Côte d'Ivoire Mon Artisan (founded 2019, expanded to Burkina Faso 2022) connects artisans with work via WhatsApp, web, and phone hotline. Over 700 artisans onboarded, 500 active. Charges 15% commission per job secured. Pre-finances projects at 0% interest via bank credit line. Customers book within 90–120 minutes. No competition in Francophone West Africa. Wima's advantage: Mon Artisan finds clients for artisans. Wima already has the clients — adding the craftsmen to an existing demand pipeline.

The 6-Step Partnership Model

1
Craftsman applies via WhatsApp
Sends 3–5 portfolio photos, phone number, city, and speciality. No formal documents required at first. Low barrier by design.
2
Wima vets with site visit or video call
Senior Wima team member reviews one completed project in person or via video. 5-point quality checklist applied. Once approved: "Wima Verified Partner" status awarded publicly.
3
Wima builds the craftsman's digital profile
WhatsApp catalogue page, Instagram story highlights (before/after), professional one-page PDF profile. Craftsman is now visible to Wima's entire client base including diaspora.
4
Wima receives and qualifies all enquiries
Wima manages all client communication. Prepares BOQ with craftsman's input. Arranges bank financing if required. Craftsman never negotiates price or chases leads.
5
Craftsman executes. Wima documents.
Craftsman does the skilled work. Wima photographs before/after, manages the client update log, and processes all payments. Portfolio grows with every Wima project.
6
Revenue split — paid within 3 days via MoMo
Wima earns 15–20%. Craftsman earns 80–85% paid within 3 days of milestone completion. MoMo disbursement. No cash handling. No invoice writing. No waiting.

Key Advertising Terms — Plain English

CPM — Cost Per Mille
What you pay for every 1,000 times your ad is shown to people — whether they click or not. Mille = Latin for 1,000. Measures reach and exposure. A CPM of $1.00 means 1,000 people saw your ad for $1. Ghana CPM: $0.30–$1.50, meaning Wima can reach 1,000 Accra homeowners for as little as GHS 4.50. Use CPM-optimised campaigns for awareness — getting people to know Wima exists before launch.
CPC — Cost Per Click
What you pay each time someone clicks your ad. Africa average CPC: $0.24 — the lowest of any major region globally. Ghana/West Africa estimated: $0.20–$0.50 per click. Compare: the US averages $1.67 CPC. The same GHS 500 budget gets Wima 5–10× more clicks than it would get a UK advertiser. Use CPC-optimised campaigns when the goal is traffic to a WhatsApp link or landing page.
CPA — Cost Per Acquisition
What you pay per result — the result being whatever you define as a conversion: a WhatsApp message, a BOQ request, a phone call, or a sale. If Wima spends GHS 500 and gets 25 WhatsApp leads, the CPA is GHS 20 per lead. CPA tells you whether the campaign is profitable. At GHS 20 CPA and GHS 1,500 average project margin, the return on ad spend is 75:1. Also called CPL (Cost Per Lead) when the acquisition event is a lead.
CPL — Cost Per Lead
A specific form of CPA where the conversion event is a lead — a name, phone number, or WhatsApp enquiry — rather than a completed sale. Global Facebook average CPL: $18.75 (Nov 2025). Ghana estimate: GHS 15–60 per WhatsApp lead, depending on targeting quality and creative. The research benchmark: if Wima's CPL exceeds GHS 100, the campaign targeting or creative needs to be revised immediately.
CTR — Click-Through Rate
The percentage of people who saw your ad and then clicked it. If 1,000 people see the ad and 20 click, CTR = 2%. A CTR above 1.49% on Facebook means Wima's ad is outperforming most competitors. Below 0.72% means the creative or audience is wrong. CTR is the first signal of whether an ad is resonating — check it after 48 hours. High CTR + low CPC = efficient campaign. Low CTR = fix the creative, not the budget.
ROAS — Return On Ad Spend
Revenue earned per GHS spent on advertising. ROAS of 3.0 means GHS 3 back for every GHS 1 spent. The global Facebook average ROAS is 2.79. For Wima's construction commerce model — where one project can generate GHS 1,500–30,000 in margin — even a ROAS of 10–50x is achievable once the sales pipeline is optimised. ROAS is the final campaign health metric that determines whether to increase budget.
