1
Go to Meta Business Suite
Open business.facebook.com on a desktop or laptop (not your phone — the full Ads Manager only works properly on desktop). Log in with the Facebook account connected to your Wima Business Page. You will see the Meta Business Suite dashboard. On the left sidebar, click Ads → then Ads Manager.
2
Create a new campaign
In Ads Manager, click the green + Create button. A campaign creation window opens. You will be asked to choose an Objective.

For this week's flyer campaign, choose: Engagement → Messages
This is the objective that creates a Click-to-WhatsApp button on your ad. When someone taps the button, WhatsApp opens directly with Wima's number pre-filled. No website needed.

Name your campaign: Wima Deal Hub — Week 1 Flyers

Click Continue.
Why Messages, not Traffic or Awareness? You are not sending people to a website — you are sending them directly into a WhatsApp conversation. "Messages" objective tells Meta to find people most likely to send a WhatsApp message. It is the correct objective for a Click-to-WhatsApp campaign.
3
Set your audience (Ad Set level)
You are now at the Ad Set level. This is where you define WHO sees your ad.

Messaging app: Select WhatsApp
Location: Ghana → Greater Accra Region (type "Greater Accra" in the location field)
Age: 28 to 55
Gender: All
Detailed targeting: Click "Add interests" and add:
  • Home improvement
  • Interior design
  • Real estate (Ghana)
  • Property renovation
  • Construction
Placements: Select Advantage+ Placements (let Meta decide — this is cheaper than manual placement)

Budget: Set a Daily budget of GHC 71 (approximately $4.70). This runs the campaign for 7 days = GHC 500 total.
Audience size tip: Check the estimated audience size on the right — it should show 200,000–800,000 people. If it shows under 100,000, broaden your interests. If it shows over 2 million, narrow your location to just Accra city.
4
Create your ad (Ad level) — upload the flyer
You are now at the Ad level. This is the actual creative — what people will see.

Ad format: Single image
Upload: Upload the flyer screenshot for the category you are running first. Start with the Acoustic Ceiling flyer (Category 01).

Primary text (the caption under the image — copy this):
Acoustic ceiling panels — supplied and installed across Accra. From GHS 85/m².

✅ No cracking
✅ Sound absorption
✅ One working day
✅ Free itemised BOQ in 24 hours

WhatsApp us your ceiling area and location. We'll send your price today. 👇
Headline: Free BOQ — 24-hour turnaround
Call to action button: Select Send WhatsApp message
WhatsApp number: Enter your Wima WhatsApp Business number

Click Preview to see how the ad will look on a phone screen. Check that the flyer is readable and the WhatsApp button is visible.
Image specs: Facebook recommends 1080×1080px for feed images. The flyer screenshot from a desktop browser will work, but for best quality, use the browser's zoom function to make the flyer fill the screen before screenshotting.
5
Review and publish
Click Review to see a full summary of your campaign. Check:

• Campaign objective: Engagement → Messages ✓
• WhatsApp app selected ✓
• Audience: Greater Accra, age 28–55 ✓
• Daily budget: GHC 71 ✓
• Ad creative: flyer uploaded ✓
• CTA button: Send WhatsApp message ✓

If everything looks correct, click Publish. Meta will review your ad — this usually takes 24 hours but often less than 2 hours. Once approved, the ad goes live and you will see it in Ads Manager with status "Active".
Do not edit the campaign after it goes live for the first 7 days. Meta's algorithm needs 7 full days to exit its learning phase. Any edit before Day 7 resets the learning and wastes everything spent so far.

Section 03 WhatsApp Setup

Setting up WhatsApp
to receive every lead.

WhatsApp does not run paid ads on its own platform. All WhatsApp lead generation happens through Facebook's Click-to-WhatsApp format (set up in Section 02 above). This section covers the WhatsApp Business setup that must be in place before any ad goes live.

WhatsApp Business setup checklist
A
Download WhatsApp Business
Download the free WhatsApp Business app (not the regular WhatsApp) from the Play Store or App Store. Use a separate phone number from your personal WhatsApp — ideally a dedicated SIM for Wima. Register with your Wima business name.
B
Complete your business profile
Go to Settings → Business tools → Business profile. Fill in: Business name: Wima Deal Hub · Category: Building Materials · Address: Accra, Ghana · Website: your GitHub Pages URL · Description: "Acoustic ceiling, drywall, exterior cladding, and insulation — supplied and installed across Ghana. Free BOQ in 24 hours."
C
Set up the 4 auto-replies
Go to Settings → Business tools → Greeting message. Turn it ON. Copy and paste the auto-reply for whichever category the ad is running. The greeting message fires automatically when someone messages Wima for the first time — or after 14 days of inactivity. Test it: send a WhatsApp from another phone and confirm the greeting arrives within 3 seconds.
D
Link WhatsApp to your Facebook Page
In Facebook Business Suite → go to your Wima Business Page → Settings → WhatsApp → enter your WhatsApp Business number → tap Send code → enter the verification code → confirm. Once linked, Meta can use your WhatsApp number as a CTA destination in ads.

Section 04 Campaign Payment & Launch

How to pay for
your Facebook campaign.

Meta (Facebook) bills all advertising in US dollars — even when your ads run in Ghana targeting Ghanaian audiences. You cannot pay with MoMo or a GHS bank account. This section tells you exactly how to fund your Meta ad account from Ghana, step by step.

Critical — read this first Meta does not accept MTN MoMo, GHS bank transfers, or Vodafone Cash. You need a US-dollar-denominated Visa or Mastercard. The most accessible solution for someone in Accra right now is a Wise virtual card — which you can open online in under 30 minutes using your Ghana phone number and a Ghana ID. This is the method used by thousands of Ghanaian digital advertisers.

Option A — Wise virtual card (recommended · fastest)
1
Open a Wise account
Go to wise.com on your phone or laptop. Click Register. Sign up with your email address. Wise accepts Ghana phone numbers and Ghana passport or Ghana Card as ID. You will need to upload a photo of your ID and take a selfie — the verification usually completes within a few hours.
Why Wise? Wise gives you a real USD account number and a virtual Visa debit card. You can fund it from your Ghana MoMo or local bank account at the real mid-market exchange rate — significantly better than bureau de change rates. Many Accra-based digital advertisers use Wise as their Meta payment card.
2
Get your Wise virtual Visa card
Once your Wise account is verified, go to Cards in the Wise app. Tap Get a card. Choose Virtual card (free — physical card costs a small fee but is not needed for Meta). Your virtual Visa card details appear immediately — card number, expiry date, and CVV. Save these securely.
Fund the card: In Wise, click Add money → select GHS → choose Bank transfer or Mobile money. Transfer the equivalent of $35–$40 USD to cover Week 2's GHC 500 budget plus a small buffer. Wise will show you the exact GHS amount at the current rate before you confirm.
3
Add the Wise card to Meta Ads Manager
In Meta Ads Manager, click the menu (≡) → Billing → Payment methods → Add payment method → Credit or debit card.

Enter your Wise virtual card details:
• Card number (16 digits from the Wise app)
• Expiry date
• CVV (3 digits)
• Billing name: your name as registered on Wise
• Billing address: your Ghana address

Click Save. Meta will charge a small verification amount (usually $0.01–$1.00) to confirm the card — this is refunded automatically.
If your card is declined: This sometimes happens because Wise needs to be set up to allow international transactions. In the Wise app, go to Cards → your virtual card → Settings → make sure "International transactions" and "Online payments" are both enabled.

Meta billing — how and when you are charged Meta bills in one of two ways: either when your spend reaches your billing threshold (starts at $25, increases as your account matures) OR at the end of your billing cycle (monthly). If your Wise card runs low and the campaign pauses, the learning phase resets — avoid this by keeping at least $20 buffer on the card at all times.
